Seating

Chair No. 1

An exploration of joinery, material tension, and profile. Chair No. 1 features a solid white oak frame with hand-shaped cylindrical back supports that lock the backrest into place. A single continuous piece of premium vegetable-tanned leather forms the seat, suspended across the frame to establish a firm yet responsive sit. Built with traditional mortise-and-tenon joints, this piece is designed to age gracefully, with the leather developing a rich patina over time.

Materials White Oak, Vegetable-Tanned Leather
Dimensions W 19" x D 20" x H 31" (Seat H 18")
