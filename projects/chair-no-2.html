Chair No. 2

A sculptural study in color and geometry. Chair No. 2 is crafted from bent plywood finished in a sky blue lacquer, contrasting with a seat lined in natural cork. The dramatic waterfall front edge cascades downwards, creating an asymmetrical silhouette that shifts as you walk around the piece. The interior volume serves as both a structural anchor and a playful storage cavity.

Materials Lacquered Bent Plywood, Natural Cork
Dimensions W 21" x D 18" x H 28" (Seat H 17.5")
